Europejska Akademia Lotnictwa

A school, platform, and community for the people who keep European aviation airborne.

Certification. Software. Talent. Built by people who work in hangars, not just around them.

What we're building

Four parts of one system, built for the hangar floor.

01

Certification pathways — supporting mechanics through Part 66 and Part 147 qualification across the EU.

02

Digital tools for maintenance operations — because the hangar deserves better software.

03

Talent and education — structured programs for the next generation of aviation engineers.

04

Community — connecting the people who do the technical work that keeps aviation safe.
