Europejska Akademia Lotnictwa

A school, platform, and community for the people who keep European aviation flying.

Certification. Software. Talent. Led by people who work in hangars.

Our mission

Every mechanic, at every stage.

We guide aircraft mechanics at every stage of their journey — from first steps in aviation to a full Aircraft Maintenance Licence (AML). Whether you're starting from zero, already hold Certificates of Recognition (CoR), or need experience at MROs to finalise your licence, we help you navigate the path.

What we're building

Four connected pillars, all designed for the hangar floor.

01

Certification pathways

Clear, structured route from zero to the Aircraft Maintenance Licence license. We support mechanics who already have CoRs from accredited EASA-approved partners, and help them find the practical experience and guidance they need to complete their AML.

02

Digital tools for maintenance

Modern, practical software for maintenance operations. We build tools that match the reality of the hangar — smarter workflows, better documentation, and integrations that work for engineers.

03

Talent and education

Programs for new candidate and upskilling for experienced mechanics. From foundational training to advanced specialisation, we focus on building confident, competent aviation engineers ready for real-world operations.

04

Community

A network for the people who keep aircraft safe and serviceable. We connect mechanics, engineers, instructors, and MROs, creating a shared space for knowledge, opportunities, and professional growth.
